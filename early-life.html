Early Life

Mr. Lad was born into a lower-middle-class family in Western Suburbs of Lower Parel. His father was a Mazgaon Dock Worker and his mother was a Government Employee. Their family home was CGHS Quarters which was only 180 sq.ft. We can understand Mr. Lad's source of humbleness and down to earth behavior. He knows how to stay connected to his grounds.

Mr. Prasad Lad married Neeta Lad in 1990. This was a phase where he decided to be an entrepreneur and launch his car rental services in 1991. He took a loan to buy his first vehicle through the help of Maharashtra government scheme for unemployed youth.
